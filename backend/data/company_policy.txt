ABC Technologies Company Policy

Customer satisfaction is our highest priority.

Refund Policy
- Refund requests must be reviewed by a human supervisor.
- Refund approval depends on eligibility and purchase history.

Subscription Cancellation
- Cancellation requests require supervisor approval.
- Active subscriptions remain valid until the end of the billing cycle.

Account Closure
- Account closure requests require identity verification.
- Human approval is mandatory before permanent deletion.

Monday to Friday
9:00 AM – 6:00 PM

Response Time

Critical technical issues receive priority handling.
